WHAT WE DO


Our mission is to advance sustainable hydropower by building and sharing knowledge on its role in renewable energy systems, responsible agro-social management and climate change solutions.

OPERATE, MANAGE, AND SELL POWER

We operate and manage. More often than not, we own the operation and sell power. We believe in local, sustainable, stable power generation using renewable hydropower.

Building Agricultural Parks

Providing basic infrastructure and services for the development of production and agricultural processing services and products.

WORK WITH COMMUNITIES

We collaborate with cities and towns, private owners, river advocates , permitting authorities, and governments.

Advocating for Climate change

Crescent Energy is working to build knowledge on renewable hydropower's contribution to climate mitigation and adaption.

About Us

Company Background

Crescent Energy is a startup organization aiming at accelerating access to electricity in building next generations mini grids with Agri-business Parks. We develop, operate, and manage small-scale, hydro facilities. Responding to the need for green energy, we are a catalyst in the renewable energy market.

To accomplish this at the highest professional level, Crescent Energy has assembled a leadership team from industry, government affairs, and technology entrepreneurship. With a mix of skills and experience derived from the energy sector and other businesses, we are devoted to the notion that a sound foundation in knowledge, a visionary approach to what is possible, and a relentless work ethic will lead our success.
